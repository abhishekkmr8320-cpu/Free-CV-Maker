Our Story
Why We Built PureFreeCV

Resume builders have always been behind a paywall. The pattern is familiar: a free trial that locks your download, a subscription to access decent templates, a credit card required just to see what your resume looks like. For millions of job seekers — especially freshers, students, and career-changers — that's not a minor inconvenience. It's a real barrier.

PureFreeCV started from a straightforward question: what if a resume builder just worked, completely free, with no strings attached? No sign-up. No trial. No paywalled exports. Every template, every feature, every PDF download — free forever.

We built everything directly in the browser using localStorage, so your resume data never leaves your device. You own your data completely. We never see it, never store it on a server, and never sell it. The tool works offline, works on mobile, and produces clean, ATS-ready PDFs every time.

Today PureFreeCV offers 15 professionally designed templates, a real-time ATS Score Checker, a Cover Letter builder, a Job Application Tracker, and multi-CV version management — all at zero cost. That's the promise, and we intend to keep it.

Our Principles
The Values Behind Every Decision

These aren't marketing words. They're the rules we hold ourselves to when building PureFreeCV.

1
Free means free
Every feature is available without payment, subscription, or sign-up — now and in the future. If we ever introduce optional paid features, the core tool will remain completely free.
2
Your data belongs to you
Resume data lives in your browser's localStorage — not our servers. We have no business model that requires owning your data, and we intend to keep it that way.
3
ATS-first, always
Every template is designed to pass ATS systems, not just to look good. If a design choice would break ATS parsing, it doesn't ship. Period.
4
Accessibility over aesthetics
We prioritise usability for everyone — freshers with no experience, professionals switching careers, people on slow connections, and mobile-first users in emerging markets.
5
Transparent about everything
We use Google Analytics for insights and Google AdSense to fund the service. We say so openly in our Privacy Policy. No hidden trackers, no dark patterns.
