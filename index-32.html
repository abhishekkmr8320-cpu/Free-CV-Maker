Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

🍪 Short version: We use a small number of essential and analytics cookies. We also serve Google AdSense ads which use their own cookies. You can opt out at any time.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, measure traffic, and — in the case of advertising cookies — show you relevant ads. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information on their own.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie / Service Type Purpose Duration
localStorage (cv-data, cv-jobs, cv-list) Essential Saves your resume data, job tracker entries and saved CVs locally in your browser. Data never leaves your device. Until you clear browser data
Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) Analytics Measures page visits, time-on-site, and feature usage to help us improve PureFreeCV. Data is anonymised. Up to 2 years
Google AdSense (IDE, DSID, etc.) Advertising Serves contextual advertisements that keep PureFreeCV free to use. Google may use these cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing history. Up to 13 months

3. Why We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies — required for the tool to function. Without localStorage, your resume data cannot be saved between sessions.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand which features are used most and where users run into problems, so we can improve the product.
  • Advertising cookies — allow us to earn revenue through ads so we can keep PureFreeCV completely free. We do not control which ads Google shows.

4. Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

You can also opt out of Google's personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com and opt out of Google Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Note: disabling essential localStorage will prevent PureFreeCV from saving your resume data between sessions.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Google AdSense and Google Analytics are third-party services. Their cookie practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy. We have no control over cookies set by Google.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in the cookies we use or changes in applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always show when it was last revised.
