1. Who We Are

PureFreeCV (purefreecv.online) is a free, browser-based CV and resume builder created and operated by Abhishek Krishnaut. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices in relation to it.

By using PureFreeCV, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the service.

2. Data We Collect

We distinguish between two types of data:

  • Resume data — the personal information you type into the CV builder (name, email, phone number, work history, education, skills, etc.). This data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage data — anonymous information about how you interact with the website, collected automatically via Google Analytics (page views, session duration, feature usage, device type, general geographic region). This data does not identify you personally.

We do not collect or store:

  • Passwords (no account system exists)
  • Payment information (PureFreeCV is 100% free)
  • Your resume files on any server
  • Precise location data

3. How Your Resume Data Is Stored

Every field you fill in — name, work history, skills, education, and more — is saved to your browser's localStorage. This is a standard browser feature that stores data on your own device, not on any remote server.

This means:

  • Your resume data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use a sharing feature.
  • PureFreeCV cannot access, read, or recover your resume data from our end.
  • If you clear your browser cache or localStorage, your resume data will be permanently deleted from that device. We cannot restore it.
  • Your data is private to the browser and device you use. It is not synced between devices.
Tip: Use the "Download PDF" or "Download DOCX" buttons regularly to keep a copy of your resume. Your browser storage is not a permanent backup.

4. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use PureFreeCV so we can improve the product. Google Analytics collects data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Approximate country and city (not street-level)
  • Referral source (how you found us)
  • Which features were used (anonymised event data)

This data is anonymised and aggregated. Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to track sessions. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use any other analytics or tracking services.

5. Advertising

PureFreeCV displays advertisements served by Google AdSense to help fund the free service. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity across the web.

We do not share your resume content or personal data with Google AdSense. The ad service operates independently based on your general browsing behaviour, not your CV data.

You can manage your Google ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

6. Third-Party Services

PureFreeCV uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — ad serving. Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Pages — website hosting. Cloudflare may log IP addresses for security purposes. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request. Google Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party for marketing purposes, and we never will.

7. Your Rights

Because your resume data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it at all times:

  • Access: Open your browser's developer tools (F12 → Application → localStorage) to view your stored data at any time.
  • Deletion: Clear your browser's localStorage or site data to permanently delete all resume data. You can also use your browser's privacy settings to manage this.
  • Portability: Download your resume as a PDF or DOCX at any time using the export buttons in the app.

For any data concerns related to Google Analytics or AdSense, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy and use Google's privacy controls.

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you may have additional rights under the GDPR including the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data held by third-party services. Contact those services directly to exercise these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

PureFreeCV is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our service, please contact us and we will take steps to address the situation.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of PureFreeCV after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
